## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account registration
- Access to your email inbox to verify your account
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

<Callout kind="info">

Mar Key works best with JavaScript enabled. Disable any ad blockers that might interfere with the signup process.

</Callout>

## Sign Up for Your Mar Key Account

Follow these steps to create your account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://app.markey.com/signup` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email address, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Mar Key and click the confirmation link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Log in and select your workspace preferences during the short onboarding quiz.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a dedicated space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout">
    From the main dashboard, click the `+ New Space` button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select visibility (`Public` or `Private`), and choose a template if desired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="arrow-right">
    Click `Create Space`. You'll land in the editor interface ready to add content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Initial Content to Your Space

Populate your space with your first page. Use Markdown for rich formatting or import from other sources.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Editor" icon="edit-3">

    Create a new page and use MDX syntax for components.

````mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: Your first page.
---

## Hello World

Welcome to Mar Key!
````

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">

    Connect your repo for seamless sync.

    1. Go to `Space Settings > Integrations`.
    2. Authorize GitHub and select your repo.
    3. Map branches to docs paths.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="AI Assistant" icon="zap">

    Generate content instantly.

````javascript
// Use the AI prompt in the editor sidebar
prompt: "Write an introduction for a SaaS product"
````

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">

Save frequently with `Cmd/Ctrl + S`. Preview changes in real-time using the split view toggle.

</Callout>
