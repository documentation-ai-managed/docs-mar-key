---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Resolve login issues with mar key dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- Stuck at login screen
- Page keeps reloading

## Common Causes

- Browser cache issues
- Incorrect password

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Try refreshing or clearing cookies/cache, then relogin.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Use the "Forgot password?" link on the login page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
