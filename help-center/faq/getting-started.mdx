## Overview

To begin using mar key, create a documentation space for your project.

## Steps

1. Login at `https://dashboard.markey.app`
2. Click "New Space"
3. Name your space and set visibility
4. Click "Create"

Your new documentation space is ready! Start adding content from your dashboard.
