## Overview

Create and manage a new project documentation space in mar key.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Log in, then click "Projects" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Click the "Add Project" button and enter the project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your project">
    Press "Create" to finalize setup. Begin adding documentation!
  </Step>
</Steps>
